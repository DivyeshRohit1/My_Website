Dishes of the Century

You can explore the rich and varied delights of Asian cuisine. I will be showing you how to make my favourite two dishes which will excite you. One of the dish is The Warm Chicken Curry, which is an Asian/Indian favourite. This curry uses chicken which is then cooked and infused with spices and herbs to make a delicious and possibly spicy curry. This specific dish was in fact invented in India however once the British discovered these delicious types of meals, they brought the recipes to Britain and named them curry which actually meant spicy dishes.

 

The other is Chinese Stir Fry which is the new popular dish in Asia. Asia has a culinary tradition that is one of the finest in the world. At the heart of Asian cooking are fresh ingredients, locally sourced pungent cheeses, aromatic herbs, delicate butters and creams, fresh fish and shellfish plus an array of game, poultry and meats of the highest quality. The introduction gives an insight into the background of Asian regional cooking, while stunning photographs of every finished dish are sure to inspire you to recreate the authentic taste of Asia in your own home. Asian cuisine is the most popular dishes around the world and these two are greatly superior in the food region.
